Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application Cosmic Plasma ("the App"), developed by GPS Gaming. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects only the following information:

  • Player name — entered voluntarily by you for display on the in-game leaderboard.
  • Game score — your total plasma score, submitted to the leaderboard.

We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, location, device identifiers, or any other personal information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your chosen name and score on the in-game global leaderboard.
  • No other use. Your data is not used for advertising, analytics, or profiling.

3. Data Storage

Leaderboard data (name + score) is stored using Google Firebase Firestore, a cloud database service operated by Google LLC. Firebase's privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

All other game data (progress, upgrades, settings) is stored locally on your device only and never transmitted.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties, except as required to operate Firebase (Google). We have no advertising partners and run no third-party SDKs beyond Firebase.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your leaderboard entry (name + score) remains in the database until you request deletion. To delete your entry, contact us at the email below with the player name you used, and we will remove it within 7 days.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.

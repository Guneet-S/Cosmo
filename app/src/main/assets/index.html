📖 HOW TO PLAY

⚛ Plasma Energy

Tap the plasma orb to generate energy. Buy generators to produce plasma automatically. Your plasma per second increases as you acquire more generators.

⚡ Generators

10 unique generators unlocking as you play. Long-press to buy max. Higher-tier generators produce exponentially more!

  • ✦ Spark: The beginning of energy.
  • 🔥 Ember: Concentrated plasma heat.
  • ☀️ Solar Flare: Star-level energy emission.
  • ⚡ Plasma Arc: Controlled energy lightning.
  • 🌀 Fusion Core: Sustainable nuclear fusion.
  • 💥 Nova Burst: Exploding star remnants.
  • 🌟 Pulsar: Rapid rotating beacon.
  • 🌌 Quasar: Supermassive black hole jet.
  • 🔮 Nebula Forge: Birthplace of stars.
  • 🕳️ Singularity: Infinite gravity point.

🔧 Upgrades

One-time purchases that boost your tap power or generator output. New upgrades unlock as you progress through the game.

🌌 Prestige

Once you reach 1M+ total plasma, you can prestige to reset your generators and earn Cosmic Shards. Each shard gives a permanent +5% boost to ALL production.

💎 Shard Upgrades

Spend Cosmic Shards on permanent upgrades: increased tap power, auto-tapper, head start plasma, and more. These persist across prestiges.

🏆 Leaderboard

Compete against other players globally! Your score is your total plasma earned across all time. Prestige to push your score to the leaderboard.
